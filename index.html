Why Manage Food Waste?

Food waste is a major issue globally, and we can all contribute to reducing it. This website helps you log and monitor food waste to adopt better habits.

Tips to Reduce Food Waste

  • Plan meals ahead of time to avoid overbuying.
  • Store food properly to extend its shelf life.
  • Use leftovers creatively in new meals.
  • Compost organic waste instead of discarding it.
